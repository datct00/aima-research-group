About AIMA

Advancing Trustworthy AI for Healthcare

Our lab focuses on advancing artificial intelligence methods for scientific discovery and real-world impact. We develop principled machine learning and deep learning approaches for challenging healthcare settings.

Key challenges include annotation scarcity, domain shift, subpopulation imbalance, uncertainty, and clinically realistic deployment constraints.
