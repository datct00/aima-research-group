Research Interests

Robust & Data-Efficient Learning in Medical AI

We develop robust and data-efficient AI methods for healthcare, focusing on learning under imperfect and limited supervision such as annotation scarcity, domain shift, and noisy acquisition conditions.

Medical Image Computing & Foundation Models

We study segmentation, reconstruction, registration, and classification across MRI, CT, ultrasound, and microscopy, with emphasis on foundation models and multimodal learning.

Translating AI into Clinical Impact

Through rigorous benchmarking and principled model design, we bridge machine learning innovation and real-world clinical deployment with reliable and interpretable systems.
